# Hello World

This is my first blog post. Welcome to my corner of the internet.

## What is this?

This blog is a retro-styled personal space where I write about things that interest me. The design is inspired by vintage computing — because old computers had soul.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about:

- **Technology** — software, hardware, and the spaces between
- **Building things** — projects, tools, and lessons learned
- **Ideas** — thoughts on where things are headed

## Why retro?

The future of computing was promised to us on beige plastic and CRT phosphor. This design is a reminder that technology used to feel *tangible* — something you could touch, hear, and smell. The whir of a floppy drive, the click of a mechanical keyboard, the warm glow of a CRT.

Let's keep that spirit alive.
